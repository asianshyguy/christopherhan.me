Destination Stamps

July 2017 | Illustrator, After Effects

I was really in a mood to travel, but I was stuck in LA TA'ing a summer course. It was a bummer, but I got to vicariously travel to these places through these stamps.
This project was really a study on Illustrator, so I decided to keep the motion simple and fun.

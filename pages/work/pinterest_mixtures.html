Work Bio
Pinterest Mixtures

March 2018 | After Effects + Illustrator

This project was a submission for the 2018 Cannes Future Lions competition organized by AKQA. I worked with a classmate in designing a solution that “was not possible 3 years ago.”
In response to this brief, we decided to tackle the global problem of food waste, leveraging the Pinterest app’s improved computer vision technology. The idea was to create a product that improved the resourcefulness of the population and encouraged users to use everything they have in their fridge and beyond.
We worked through the ideation phase together, developing the product’s design. Then, Caitlin developed the communication angle we wanted to take, and I animated a motion graphics piece to illustrate the benefits of Pinterest Mixtures to the public.

Work Bio
Airbnb

Fall 2017 | After Effects + Illustrator

This project was my first submission for the 2016 Cannes Future Lions competition organized by AKQA. I worked with a classmate in designing a solution that “was not possible 3 years ago.” We worked through the ideation phase together, developing the product’s design. Then, Caitlin designed the communication angle we wanted to take, and I animated a motion graphics piece to illustrate our idea.
At the time of development, Airbnb had just started testing their Experiences Marketplace, where travelers could sign up for guided tours of cities, nature hikes, food & drink tastings, and more. This provided hosts an opportunity to welcome and immerse their guests and beyond the confines of their homes. While most of the service has been used for recreational use, Caitlin and I thought it was a great opportunity to leverage the Airbnb network to organize community improvement events.

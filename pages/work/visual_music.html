Experiments in Visual Music

Fall 2017 | After Effects + Illustrator + Houdini

These are some of my sketches from my class with Mike Patterson, who created this.
The class focussed on concepts of abstraction and expression through motion. We studied choreography with international ballet masters and learned musical concepts such as counterpoint and rhythm. All this with the intention to implement the creative frameworks behind other mediums into our own motion.
I undertook the ambitious process of familiarizing myself with SideFX Houdini. Here is the portion of my piece that I showcased in class. It implements a custom VEX particle system that is asynchronous with the music, but retimed in after effects to be loosely aligned:

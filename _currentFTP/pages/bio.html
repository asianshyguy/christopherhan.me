I am a multidisciplinary creative, focused on integrating design thinking principles into storytelling. An inaugural student of the Jimmy Iovine & Andre Young (Dr Dre) Academy, I studied the intersections of art, technology, and business in innovation. Working with interdisciplinary teams of my classmates, I developed a spirit of collaborative, cross-disciplinary communication.

Within this academic framework, I concentrated my studies on visual storytelling and audio design. The first was designed to integrate visual design, creative writing, and technical software proficiency (AE, C4D, Maya, Houdini, C++). This training in all facets of the creative storytelling process, coupled with the interdisciplinary mindset of the Academy, has developed my ability to translate between the various disciplines involved throughout a production or design project.

My ultimate dream is to work with a team of creators to tell the stories of under-served populations and cultures underrepresented in media, while pushing the boundaries of the visual storytelling medium at-large. With my cross-disciplinary skillset, I am prepared to wear many hats and support the team in whatever capacity best supports the story.

Bio

I’m a motion graphics storyteller that integrates various animation software packages and technologies to compose compelling and unique stories. I am most passionate about the empathetic research behind stoytelling, especially for stories that fall within the narrative non-fiction genre. My dream is to work with an interdisciplinary team of creators to tell the stories of underserved populations and cultures new to me, while pushing the boundaries of the visual storytelling medium.

I am part of the inaugural class of the Iovine & Young Academy for arts, technology, and the business of innovation. The program’s focus on cross-disciplinary education and problem solving inspires my interdisciplinary approach to animation & storytelling. I bring a design-thinking mindset to my work, trying to understand the story’s visual needs before deciding upon the style and tools I will use to tell that story.

The bread & butter of my animation toolkit are After Effects and Illustrator, but Cinema 4D and Houdini are my 3D tools of choice. Other tools I commonly use on projects include: Maya, Photoshop, FCPX, Premiere Pro, and Logic Pro X (sound design). The new "toys" I’ve been playing with are Touch Designer, Processing, and Sketch App.
